About Us

Kappa Theta Pi (KTP) is a leading professional fraternity uniting individuals with a passion for technology and innovation. Our goal is to cultivate a community of future tech leaders by fostering growth, camaraderie, and professional development. Our mission is to equip members with the resources, mentorship, and support they need to thrive in the tech industry. We are dedicated to excellence, inclusivity, and the continuous pursuit of knowledge.

President's Message

Kappa Theta Pi at the University of Texas at Austin is the university's premier co-ed technology fraternity, dedicated to fostering the technical, professional, and social growth of its members while giving back to the community through impactful philanthropic efforts.

Since its inception, KTP has been a supportive community of like-minded individuals who uplift one another both professionally and personally. Through a variety of technical, professional, social, and community service events, KTP creates immense value for its members, helping them build lifelong networks of professionals and friends.

Beyond your time on campus, the brotherhood continues for life, offering enduring friendships and a clearer sense of your career aspirations. Join KTP today and become part of a community that shapes both your future and the lives of those around you!

Our Mission

We strive to build a community of future leaders in tech, fostering growth, friendship, and professional development. Thus, our mission is to empower members to achieve their potential in the tech industry by providing resources, mentorship, and a supportive community.
