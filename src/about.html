About Us

Kappa Theta Pi (KTP) is a leading professional fraternity uniting individuals with a passion for technology and innovation. Our goal is to cultivate a community of future tech leaders by fostering growth, camaraderie, and professional development.

Our mission is to equip members with the resources, mentorship, and support they need to thrive in the tech industry. We are dedicated to excellence, inclusivity, and the continuous pursuit of knowledge.
